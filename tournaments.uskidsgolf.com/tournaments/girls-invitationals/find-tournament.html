The World Championships
All roads lead to Pinehurst! Over two weeks each year, we welcome over 2,000 young golfers, ages 5-18, representing more than 50 countries.

Positive Coaching Alliance
An instrumental program for helping players, coaches, and parents realize that all sports are opportunities to learn how to become a winner on the field and in life.

U.S. Kids Golf Foundation
Since our inception in 2001, more than 1 million young golfers have been positively impacted through the efforts of the U.S. Kids Golf Foundation. Today, we run more than 2,000 tournaments worldwide.

The Short Game
An independent film company followed eight young golfers as they prepared and participated in the 2012 World Championship. The award-winning film inspires young athletes, regardless of their sport, and is highly recommended by film critics and audiences.

Mission
To help kids have fun learning the lifelong game of golf and encourage family interaction that builds lasting memories.
