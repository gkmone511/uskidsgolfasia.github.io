Location Information for European Championship 2023

With over 40 miles of stunning coastline, golden beaches, rolling countryside, award-winning attractions, excellent food & drink, and the world’s finest links golf courses, the East Lothian region features the best of Scotland!

Weather Information

The weather in Scotland in late May is unpredictable. Warm weather gear is necessary for the Championship. Conditions can range from 70 degrees and sunny to 40 degrees with rain. Players and caddies will need rain suits, wind shirts/breakers, umbrellas, towels, hand warmers, and sunblock.

    • Transportation Information

    Gullane, East Lothian, Scotland is located approximately 20 miles east of Edinburgh. It is recommended to fly into Edinburgh and then rent a car for the drive to Gullane. You should check with your insurance provider regarding your rental coverage while in Scotland.

    It is also recommended to check with your cell phone provider regarding your coverage when traveling internationally.

    Dress Code

    The dress code for the competition will reflect U.S. Kids Golf Policy. No cargo shorts will be permitted at any venue. Denim is not permitted. Clubhouse and course access will be denied to those violating the Dress Code. Tennis shoes may not be permitted at some facilities. Caps and hats should always be removed indoors at all facilities. Please review the specific dress codes for each venue before arriving (information will be available prior to the event).
