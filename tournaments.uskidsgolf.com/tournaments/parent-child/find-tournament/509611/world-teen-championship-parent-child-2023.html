World Teen Championship Parent/Child 2023

Register
Event Date(s): 
July 25, 2023

The World Teen Championship Parent/Child is a chance for parents to team up with their teenage players in a fun, modified scramble format. The 9-hole event will be a tee time start on Tuesday, July 25th at Mid Pines Inn and Golf Club and Pinehurst No. 6.

Registration: Registration will open for all registered World Teen Championship participants on Friday, June 9th. An invitation with registration information will be sent on Thursday, June 8th informing participants of the specific date and time of opening. Registration is on a first-come, first-serve basis and will be limited at each golf course. If you register for the World Teen Championship after June 9th and would like to compete in the Parent-Child, please contact tournaments@uskidsgolf.com. Registration is on a first-come, first-serve basis and will be limited at each golf course. Adult partners must be at least 18 years old and cannot compete with more than one junior golfer.

Follow the steps in the link below to register the parent/guardian as the players teammate:

Adding a Parent:

*Players must be signed up for the World Teen Championship to be eligible to compete in the Parent/Child event.

Cost: $175 per team. Entry fee includes greens fees for two players, golf cart, lunch for players, tee gift and awards.

Start time: Tee times will start at 8:00 a.m.

Format: Modified Scramble – Only parents will tee off on three designated holes and only kids will tee off on three designated holes. Competitors will scramble for the remainder of each hole. Both players will tee off on the remaining 3 holes and select the best tee shot.

Spectator Carts: There will be no spectator carts available for this event.

Age Group Yardages: Yardages for players and Parents/Guardians will be available the day of the event.

Awards: Trophies will be presented to the 1st and 2nd place teams in each age group.

Tiebreaker: U.S. Kids Golf Scorecard Playoff will be used to break ties for all awards. Winner will be determined on the basis of the team score on the last six holes, last three holes and finally the last hole. If the teams are still tied, continue backward from the last hole until a winner is determined. (The “last nine holes, last six holes, etc.” is considered to be 10-18, 13-18, etc.)

Course Assignments:

Please note: teams will play 9 holes.

Age Group Course
Boys 15-18 Mid Pines Inn and Golf Club (Back 9)
Girls 15-18 Pinehurst No. 6 (Back 9)
Boys 13-14 Mid Pines Inn and Golf Club (Front 9)
Girls 13-14 Pinehurst No. 6 (Front 9)
