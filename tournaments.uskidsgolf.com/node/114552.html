Master Kids Coaches

Â 

Since 2008, U.S. Kids Golf has named Master Kids Coaches to honor instructors for their ongoing efforts in growing the game of golf among kids. These individuals have earned the distinction of Master Kids Coach by winning the Top 50 Kids Coach Award three times.

Being named a Master Kids Coach is the highest honor an instructor can receive from U.S. Kids Golf. There are 208 teachers who have received this honor, 12 of whom joined the ranks in 2021.
